Company Profile

CV Geospatial Map Services is a leading systems provider and an information technology company that provides web-based Geographic Information System (GIS) solutions to the public and private sector in the Philippine Market. We cater to the digital transformation needs of organizations through its extensive array of geospatial services and solutions through open-source GIS to provide flexibility in achieving client objectives and system needs. CV Geospatial Map Services takes part in the widespread adaptation and utilization of spatial technology. We are committed to deliver efficient, cost-effective, high quality products, and services that follows the international ISO standards. Our people are comprised of - a group of sharp, dynamic, and highly skilled professionals with a background in MS Geomatics Engineering specializing in Geoinformatics, who thrive in a demanding environment. We work together to develop breakthrough solutions for our clients.

Key Products and Services

Provide Geographic Information System solutions tailored to client needs.
Provide mapping services of data acquired in the field and remote sensing images.
Train GIS users for efficient use of GIS software in a professional setting
Provide spatial data analysis for location-specific problems and solutions
Integrate existing databases to web-based mapping systems
